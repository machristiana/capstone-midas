Our Story

You never have a second chance at a first impression.

Contrarily, you also shouldn’t be judged for the way you look. Yet, this happens all the time. Don’t judge a book by its cover? Ha! Who doesn’t?

This is why, owner, Ella Saltoun, wanted to reframe people’s perception. She wanted to transcend the stigma of first impressions and make a line of eyewear that didn’t allow people to be judged. It allowed them to be noticed.

Meet Ella!

After having to buy new frames and lenses for her daughters constantly due to the glasses breaking, she started researching everything there was about how to manufacture her own eyewear. She understood that for eyewear, quality comes first. If there are only expensive lenses but cheap frames, it will break. Both factors need to be of the best quality, and that is why she designed her brand to be durable, fashionable, and the brand that successful people wear.

She chose the name Midas for her brand after one of her favorite Greek myths growing up. And Midas, with his golden touch, wasn’t just successful, he wasn’t just noticed, he was remembered. People who wear the Midas are never judged for how they look. They’re noticed. Midas makes sure you are what people remember.

Crown your eyes.

The Process

During our manufacturing process, we contact different manufacturers who can manufacture similar styles for our new collection. We manufacture a couple of samples from different manufactures and perform numerous quality controls. Our quality control process includes testing by the opticians and stylists we have partnerships with. Once the best design and quality is chosen, we manufacture this model and add it to the collection.
